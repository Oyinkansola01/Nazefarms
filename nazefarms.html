Welcome to the era of Modern Organic and Eco-Friendly Integrated Agriculture

Naze farms is an Integrated agricultural mega-hub located in the heart of imo state.Our revolutionary approach to family is centered around our core values of Environmental Substainability. Society impact and Continuous Improvement.

At Naze Farms we leverage on latest cutting-edge knowledge in the Biological Energy and Information Sciences to enture continuous improvement and value addition.

Our Farm Culture and Value

Our core values are the driving force behind the establishment of the Farm. We are committed to empowering and steering Nigeria towards a new era of environmentally substainable and integrated farming, leading to food security in Nigeria. In addition, our innovative approach to agriculture seeks to empower the teeming unemployed youths in Nigeria, through a clear demonstration of the strategic importance of agriculture in the economy.

Environmental Sustainability- Our integrated farming Model is at the core of our self-sustaining Agricultural System which helps farmers produce better food in larger quantities with smaller investments thereby ensuring a more viable Agri-business Solution. Emphasis is on the renewal and regeneration of the ecosystem in line with the global climate change agenda.

Societal Impact- Through the "GAIN" initiative, Naze Farms seeks to ensure significant social and economic impact by empoerin the youth with requisite technical, leadership and business skills in integrated agriculture. Of great importance is the reorientation of the mind of the youths to see the grossly under estimated tremendous value in agriculture as an end-to-end value chain business.

Continuous Improvement- By combining the very latest trends and best practices in biological, energy and informational sciences, we ensure the constant continuous improvement mindset and practice.
